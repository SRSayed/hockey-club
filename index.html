Clients Question

Welcome to our hockey club! Explore FAQs for information on memberships, events, and more. Get ready for an exciting season!

Q: How can I join the hockey club?

A: Visit our membership page, fill out the form, and attend our orientation. We welcome players of all skill levels.

Q: Are there age restrictions for joining?

A: No, our club is open to players of all ages, from youth to adults. We offer diverse programs for every age group.

Q: What equipment do I need to play?

A: Basic gear includes skates, stick, helmet, and gloves. Our club provides guidance on quality equipment for optimal performance and safety.

Q: When and where are the practices held?

A: Practice schedules vary by team and age group. Check our calendar for updates on times and locations.
